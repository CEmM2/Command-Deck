# Why Overleaf

Overleaf is a collaborative LaTeX editor in the browser.

## Why we use it

- Coauthor editing without everyone installing LaTeX locally.
- Comments and tracked changes.
- Easy sharing with collaborators.
- Built-in PDF compilation.
- Journal templates are often easy to start from.

## When local LaTeX is better

- Large generated documents.
- Automated builds.
- Heavy Git workflows.
- Complex figure pipelines.
- Offline work.

## Lab rule

Use Overleaf for collaboration. Use Git/GitHub for version control, backup, and serious project history when the paper becomes important enough to deserve adult supervision.
