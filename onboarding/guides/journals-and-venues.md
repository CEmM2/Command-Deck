## Reading a paper

For each paper, write:

- the problem;
- the method;
- the assumptions;
- the main result;
- what is reusable;
- what is suspicious;
- how it connects to the lab project.

Do not just collect PDFs. A folder of unread PDFs is not expertise. It is digital sediment.
